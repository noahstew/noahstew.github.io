About Me

My Story

I was born in Regina, Saskatchewan where I grew a love of family, friends, sports, and eventually computers and coding. Although I never learned to code until my first year of university, it was the first week of grade 12 where I knew that working in computers will bring me lots of joy in my future years. This lead me to the University of British Columbia in Kelowna, BC where in Fall 2022 I would enroll in my first year of a computer science degree. Many people take CS for the money and ability to work remotely but I am proud to say I am in it for my excitement and passion in the industry.
